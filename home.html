The Rolex Datejust 36 is a classic, elegant wristwatch known for its 36mm case size, automatic movement, and iconic date function at 3 o'clock. Introduced in 1945, it combines timeless design with modern precision, offering options in stainless steel, gold, and a variety of dial and bezel choices.

The Rolex Daytona is a legendary chronograph designed for motorsport enthusiasts. Known for its precision, it features a tachymeter scale for speed calculation, a robust stainless steel case, and a high-performance automatic movement. Its iconic design and exceptional functionality have made it one of Rolex's most coveted models.

The Rolex Sea-Dweller is a professional dive watch designed for extreme depths. With water resistance up to 1,220 meters (4,000 feet), it features a robust, helium-escape valve, a rotating bezel, and a legible dial.
